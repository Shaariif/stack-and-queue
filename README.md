Scenario 1: The Kitchen Plate Dishwasher
----------------------------------------


A family kitchen after a big dinner. The dishwasher is full of clean dishes, and the counter is
covered with dirty plates, bowls, and cups. You need to put the clean dishes away in the
cupboard to make room for the dirty ones. The clean dishes are stored in the dishwasher rack,
and the cupboard has limited space right in front where you can place items.
ToDo Operations:
1. What should you do when you take a clean dish out of the dishwasher to place it into the
cupboard?
2. How do you retrieve the dish from the cupboard when it's needed for the next meal?
3. How do you retrieve the dish from the cupboard when it's needed for the next meal?
4. How would you check if the cupboard is empty of clean dishes?

Scenario 2: The Morning Coffee Shop
-----------------------------------


A popular coffee shop during the morning rush hour. Customers are streaming in, tired and
eager to get their caffeine fix before work. The shop has a clear system: a single-entry door, a
well-defined line that snakes towards the counter, and a single exit point after you get your
order.
ToDo Operations:
1. When a new customer walks in, they join the back of the line. Which operation is
responsible for adding this new customer to the end of the line, and how does this
operation maintain the order of arrival?
2. When the barista looks to see who is next to be served, they focus on the person at the
front of the line. What queue operation allows the barista to view the customer at the
front of the line without removing them from the line ?
3. After the barista calls "Next!", the person at the front of the line moves forward to place
their order. Which operation is responsible for removing the customer from the front of
the line and allowing the rest of the customers to move up?
4. When a second barista arrives and wants to check how many customers are in the line,
what operation can they use to view the entire line from the front to the back, reflecting
the exact order of customers based on when they arrived?
